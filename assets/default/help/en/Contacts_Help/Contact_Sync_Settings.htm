Contact Options

The following settings are used to control the syncing of contacts:
When syncing contacts, it is possible for conflicts to occur where the same contact was changed (i.e. modified or deleted) on both the phone and in your online address book. If this happens, the following options define how such conflicts should be resolved:
Phone overwrites server: the online contact is overwritten by the contact from the phone.
Server overwrites phone: the contact on the phone is overwritten by the online contact.
Create duplicates: the original contact remains unchanged and a new contact containing the current details is added on both the phone and online.
Ignore conflict: neither of the conflicting contacts are changed.
