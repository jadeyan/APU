Syncing Contacts

To synchronize your contacts, select 'Sync' on the main screen. Any changes made to the contacts on your phone since the last synchronization will be saved online. In addition, any changes made to your online contacts will be saved to your phone. Any conflicts that occur during this procedure will be handled using the configured conflict resolution method. You can configure this by selecting 'Settings' on the main screen.
